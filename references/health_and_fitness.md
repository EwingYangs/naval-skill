## Methods & Strategies

- **Meditation + Exercise + Fasting** — Naval's three-pillar health system. All three work by strategic subtraction, not addition.

- **Walking meetings** — "Upgrade walking meetings to walking up and down hills if all parties are up for it." (2019-09-18) Physical + intellectual in one.

- **Living within walking distance** — "Living within walking distance of your friends completely transforms your quality of life." (2023-11-30) Design your environment for movement.

- **Intermittent fasting timing** — "When intermittent fasting, it's easier to eat healthy in the daytime than in the evening, before willpower depletion." (2021-07-24) Work with biology, not against it.

- **Habit-stacking for exercise** — Pair workouts with your favorite content. Make exercise the reward, not the cost. (2020-07-26)

- **One reason to meditate** — "To prove to yourself how little you actually need out of life." (2021-12-05) Meditation reveals the emptiness of desire.

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

- ❌ **"The most popular diet is 'tomorrow.'"** (2022-02-13) — Procrastination dressed as planning.

- ❌ **"A person who needs to be at peace will never be at peace."** (2024-03-22) — Treating peace as a goal defeats the purpose.

- ❌ **"Don't live in peace, live in truce with all of your conflicting desires."** (2023-09-21) — Accept internal conflict rather than pretending it away.

- ❌ **"How long until we create the perfect drug, that has everyone addicted, all the time?"** (2020-08-22) — Technology can destroy health as easily as improve it.

- ❌ **"Get grades, applause, titles now - emptiness later."** (2020-05-31) — Sacrificing health for status symbols.

- ❌ **Overstimulation** — The default modern state. Too much input, too little movement, too much food. The fix is subtraction.

## Philosophy of Health

- **Health as subtraction**: Naval's health philosophy is about removing bad inputs (overstimulation, overeating, overthinking), not adding supplements or protocols.

- **Paradise definition**: "Paradise is a garden, with a (water) view, within walking distance of a cafe." (2023-04-25) — Health is environmental, not heroic.

- **To see if you have free will, just stop exercising it.** (2020-08-01) — Agency starts with the body.

- **Talking about being is like thinking about walking.** (2024-11-08) — Don't analyze health, just do it.
