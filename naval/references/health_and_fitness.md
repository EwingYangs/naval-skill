# Health & Fitness

> Naval's approach to physical health. Body as foundation for wealth and happiness.
> Serves: naval-health, naval-happiness

## Core Principles

- 🔥 **"A calm mind, a fit body, a house full of love. These things cannot be bought. They must be earned."**
  - The three pillars. Health is non-negotiable for the other two.

- 🔥 **"Stay healthy, get wealthy, seek truth, give love, and create beauty."**
  - Health comes first in Naval's priority order — before wealth.

- 🔥 **Exercise is the closest thing to a miracle drug.**
  - Fixes mood, energy, sleep, focus. Most people underinvest in it.

- 🔥 **"The hardest thing is not doing something hard. It's doing something easy, every single day."**
  - Health is about daily habits, not heroic efforts.

- 🔥 **Sleep is the foundation.**
  - Poor sleep ruins judgment, mood, and health. Protect it above all.

- 🔥 **Diet is simple — eat real food.**
  - Avoid processed food, sugar, seed oils. Don't overcomplicate it.

- 🔥 **Walking is underrated.**
  - Naval walks daily. Best thinking time. Low-impact, high-reward.

- 🔥 **Meditation is mental fitness.**
  - Not spiritual — practical. Trains attention and reduces desire.

- 🔥 **"If you could choose how you would die, you'd die in meditation."**
  - The ultimate state of peace is also the healthiest state of mind.

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Outsourcing health to doctors and pills instead of daily habits
- ❌ Sacrificing sleep for productivity (net negative)
- ❌ Extreme diets and protocols — simple consistency beats complexity
- ❌ Gym as social media content instead of actual practice
- ❌ Ignoring mental health while optimizing physical health
